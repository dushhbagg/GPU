Power Requirements:

  • RTX 5090: 520W TGP - Requires 850W+ PSU with PCIe 5.0 12VHPWR connector
  • RTX 5080: 320W TGP - Requires 750W+ PSU with PCIe 5.0 12VHPWR connector
  • RTX 4090: 450W TGP - Requires 850W+ PSU with PCIe 5.0 12VHPWR connector

Always check your total system wattage and ensure your PSU has adequate headroom (20% above total system draw).

DLSS 4 (Deep Learning Super Sampling) is NVIDIA's AI-powered upscaling technology:

  • AI Upscaling: Renders at lower resolution, upscales with AI for better performance
  • Frame Generation: Creates additional frames using AI for smoother gameplay
  • Reflex Integration: Reduces input latency for competitive gaming
  • Quality Modes: Quality, Balanced, Performance, Ultra Performance

DLSS 4 can provide up to 4x performance improvement in supported games while maintaining or improving visual quality.
